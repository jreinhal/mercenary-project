  Filed: September 12, 2024
  Claim: Software patent infringement (US Patent 10,847,291 — "System and Method for
  Adaptive Document Retrieval Using Multi-Modal Query Processing")
  Plaintiff: TechPatent LLC (non-practicing entity / patent assertion entity)
  Plaintiff Counsel: Quinn Emanuel Urquhart & Sullivan LLP
  Defendant Counsel (Our Firm): Morrison & Foerster LLP (Partner: Michael Rodriguez)
  Exposure: $2.5M - $8M
  Probability of Adverse Outcome: 35% (based on preliminary prior art analysis)
  Probability-Weighted Exposure: $875K - $2.8M
  Reserve: $3.5M (conservative; includes legal costs through trial)

  Claim Analysis:
  - Patent US 10,847,291 claims a "method for processing user queries across multiple
    document types using adaptive retrieval strategies"
  - Our system (Sentinel RAG pipeline) uses AdaptiveRagService for query routing, which
    Plaintiff alleges reads on Claims 1, 4, 7, and 12 of the '291 patent
