================================================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

The Enterprise Transformation Program is a comprehensive 3-year initiative designed
to modernize our technology infrastructure, optimize business processes, and
position the organization for sustainable growth in an increasingly digital
marketplace.

PROGRAM LEADERSHIP
------------------
Executive Sponsor: Dr. Robert Chen, Chief Information Officer
Program Director: Patricia Anderson
Technical Lead: Marcus Thompson
Change Management Lead: Dr. Katherine Williams

PROGRAM OVERVIEW
----------------
Total Program Budget: $150 Million
Program Duration: January 2024 - December 2026 (36 months)
Current Status: Phase 2 In Progress
