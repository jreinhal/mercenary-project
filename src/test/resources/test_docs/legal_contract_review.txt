ENTERPRISE LICENSE AGREEMENT - KEY TERMS
================================================================================

MICROSOFT ENTERPRISE AGREEMENT (EA)
-----------------------------------
Contract Number: MSF-2024-ENT-7892
Effective Date: March 1, 2024
Term: 3 Years (through February 28, 2027)
Total Contract Value: $85.5 Million

Key Terms:
1. Pricing Protection: 3% annual cap on price increases
2. True-Up Rights: Annual true-up with 30-day cure period
3. License Mobility: Full rights to move licenses across platforms
4. Audit Rights: Microsoft may audit with 30-day notice, once per year
5. Termination: 90-day notice required for early termination
6. Data Portability: Full data export rights within 60 days of termination

Special Provisions:
- Most Favored Customer pricing clause active
- Co-marketing agreement included
- Joint innovation fund of $2 Million established

AWS ENTERPRISE DISCOUNT PROGRAM
-------------------------------
Contract Number: AWS-EDP-2024-3421
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Term: 3 Years
Committed Spend: $66.9 Million over term

Key Terms:
1. Discount Tier: Enterprise Tier (35% baseline discount)
2. Volume Commitment: $22.3 Million annual minimum
3. Overage Rates: Standard pricing applies beyond commitment
4. Reserved Instance Rights: Full flexibility to modify
5. Credits: $500K in migration credits included
