# Project NEXUS: Fiscal Year 2026 Budget
**Classification: CONFIDENTIAL**
**Department:** DEFENSE
**Date:** Oct 1, 2025

## Executive Summary
Project NEXUS has been allocated a total of **$47.3 million** for FY2026. This funding covers hardware acquisition, personnel salaries, and the new data center construction in Nevada.

## Breakdown
- **R&D:** $15.2M
- **Operations:** $12.5M
- **Personnel:** $8.8M
- **Infrastructure:** $10.8M

## Notes
The budget increase of 12% over FY2025 is due to the integration of the new AI sentinel modules. Captain Vasquez has updated the threat model which justified the additional spend on cyber-defense.
