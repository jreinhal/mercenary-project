# DEFENSE SECTOR - OPERATION DIAMOND SHIELD AFTER-ACTION REPORT

## Document ID: DEF-2025-TS-9821
## Classification: SECRET//NOFORN

### Section 1: Operation Summary

Operation Diamond Shield was a multi-domain exercise conducted November 15-22, 2025.
Exercise Director: Major General Richard Hayes, USAF
Participating forces: US Army, US Navy, US Air Force, Royal Navy, Bundeswehr

Exercise objectives:
- Validate C4ISR interoperability across coalition partners
- Test SENTINEL Platform integration with tactical systems
- Assess cyber defense posture against simulated adversary actions

### Section 2: Intelligence Integration

SENTINEL Platform designated primary OSINT aggregation tool.
Platform processed 142,847 intelligence reports during exercise.
LTC Jennifer Brooks managed Joint Intelligence Operations Center (JIOC).

Key integration milestones:
- Real-time threat correlation: 89% accuracy vs analyst baseline
- Multi-source fusion latency: 3.2 seconds average
- False positive rate: 2.1% (below 5% threshold)

### Section 3: Lessons Learned

Colonel Thomas Richardson identified 12 areas for improvement.
Major improvements needed in secure air-gap deployment procedures.
Coordination with Director Patterson (ref: OPS-2025-1242) on civilian-military interface.

Classified annex prepared by Captain Sarah Mitchell for CENTCOM review.
FedRAMP certification identified as prerequisite for permanent DOD deployment.
