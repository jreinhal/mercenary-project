MISSION BRIEFING: OPERATION BETA
Classification: UNCLASSIFIED
Department: OPERATIONS
Date: 2025-01-08

MISSION OVERVIEW:
Operation Beta is a follow-up mission to Operation Alpha. This mission focuses on securing identified supply routes and establishing permanent monitoring stations.

KEY PERSONNEL:
- Mission Commander: Colonel James Mitchell
- Engineering Lead: Major Thomas Park
- Security Team Leader: Captain Lisa Wong

OBJECTIVES:
1. Secure supply routes identified in Operation Alpha
2. Install monitoring equipment at 6 key locations
3. Train local personnel on equipment maintenance
4. Establish emergency evacuation protocols

EQUIPMENT DEPLOYED:
- Monitoring stations (x6)
- Solar power units
- Encrypted communication relays
- Motion detection sensors

COORDINATION:
This mission requires coordination with Operation Alpha findings. Colonel Mitchell will serve as commander for both operations to ensure continuity.

EXPECTED COMPLETION: February 15, 2025

END BRIEFING
