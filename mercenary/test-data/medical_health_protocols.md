# Field Medical Protocols and Health Standards
**Classification:** SECRET
**Department:** MEDICAL
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Medical Director:** Dr. Elizabeth Harper

## Overview
This document establishes medical protocols for all field operations, including Operations Alpha and Beta.

## Pre-Deployment Requirements

### Medical Clearance
All personnel must complete:
1. Physical examination within 30 days of deployment
2. Psychological evaluation
3. Immunization updates (see Appendix A)
4. Blood type verification and cross-match

### Required Immunizations
- Typhoid
- Hepatitis A & B
- Tetanus/Diphtheria booster
- Region-specific vaccines as required

## Casualty Evacuation Procedures

### Priority Categories
1. **URGENT** - Life threatening, evacuation within 1 hour
2. **PRIORITY** - Serious injury, evacuation within 4 hours
3. **ROUTINE** - Minor injury, evacuation within 24 hours

### Evacuation Routes
Coordinate with Operations for current extraction points. Reference Operation Alpha briefing for sector-specific locations.

## Reporting Requirements
- Daily health status reports
- Immediate notification for:
  - Any casualty
  - Disease outbreak
  - Environmental hazard exposure
  - Mental health concerns
