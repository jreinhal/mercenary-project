INTELLIGENCE REPORT - OPERATION SHADOW LENS
REPORT NUMBER: IR-2026-0147
DATE: January 5, 2026
CLASSIFICATION: TOP SECRET // ORCON

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
Human intelligence sources have confirmed that a foreign adversary is actively targeting members of Project NEXUS for recruitment. Three attempted approaches have been documented in the past 90 days.

DETAILS:
On December 12, 2025, Dr. Marcus Webb (Technical Lead, Project NEXUS) reported an unsolicited contact via LinkedIn from an individual claiming to represent a European technology consulting firm. Counterintelligence investigation revealed the firm is a known front for foreign intelligence services.

On December 28, 2025, a suspicious vehicle was observed conducting surveillance near the residence of Captain Elena Vasquez. License plate traced to a rental agency; renter used fraudulent identification.

On January 3, 2026, Director Sarah Chen received an encrypted email containing a phishing link. Analysis by Cyber Command confirmed the malware signature matches known APT-29 tooling.

ASSESSMENT:
Foreign intelligence services have likely obtained a partial roster of Project NEXUS personnel. The source of the leak is under investigation. All project members have been briefed on enhanced security protocols.

RECOMMENDATIONS:
1. Increase counterintelligence briefings to monthly
2. Implement additional communications security measures
3. Consider relocating key personnel to secure facilities
4. Coordinate with FBI for domestic counterintelligence support
