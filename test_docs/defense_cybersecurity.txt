# DEFENSE SECTOR - CYBERSECURITY ASSESSMENT

## Document ID: DEF-2025-TS-9892
## Classification: SECRET

### Section 1: Threat Landscape Analysis

Nation-state cyber threat assessment prepared by CYBERCOM analyst Dr. William Park.
Analysis period: October 1 - December 31, 2025
Threat actors tracked: 47 distinct APT groups with varying capability levels

Top threat profiles:
- APT-29 (Cozy Bear): Advanced persistent access, intelligence collection focus
- APT-28 (Fancy Bear): Disruptive operations capability, targeting NATO allies
- APT-41: Dual espionage/financial motivation, targeting defense contractors
- UNKNOWN-47: New actor identified during Operation Diamond Shield

### Section 2: Defensive Measures

Defense-in-depth strategy implemented per Colonel Thomas Richardson guidance.
Zero-trust architecture validated across all SENTINEL deployments.
CAC/PIV authentication enforced for all GovCloud instances.

Security controls applied:
- STIG compliance: 100% of applicable controls implemented
- Continuous monitoring: 24/7 SOC coverage with 15-minute max response
- Incident response: Mean time to contain reduced to 4 hours

### Section 3: Recommendations

LTC Jennifer Brooks (ref: DEF-2025-TS-9821) concurred with upgrade priorities.
