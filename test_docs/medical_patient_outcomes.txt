# MEDICAL SECTOR - PATIENT OUTCOMES ANALYSIS

## Document ID: MED-2025-7956
## Data Classification: HIPAA PROTECTED

### Section 1: Long-Term Follow-Up Results

Follow-up analysis for Protocol SENT-2025-001 (ref: MED-2025-7823)
Analysis conducted by Biostatistician Dr. Amanda Chen
6-month post-treatment outcomes evaluated for 412 completed participants

Outcome metrics:
- Disease-free survival: 89.4% (95% CI: 86.2-92.6%)
- Quality of life improvement: Mean 18.7 points on SF-36 scale
- Patient satisfaction: 4.2/5.0 (95% very or somewhat satisfied)

### Section 2: Subgroup Analysis

Dr. Katherine Williams requested additional stratification by demographic factors.
Analysis by Dr. Lisa Chang (ref: MED-2025-7823, Site B) showed consistent results.

Results by age group:
- Under 50 years: 42% improvement in diagnosis time
- 50-65 years: 35% improvement in diagnosis time  
- Over 65 years: 28% improvement in diagnosis time
